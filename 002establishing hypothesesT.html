50 Scenarios for One-Sample T-Test with Hypotheses

Below are 50 diverse scenarios where a one-sample t-test can be applied. For each scenario, the correct null hypothesis (H₀) and alternative hypothesis (Hₐ) are provided based on the context and wording of the test being conducted.

    Null Hypothesis (H₀): μ = 500 grams

    Alternative Hypothesis (Hₐ): μ ≠ 500 grams

  2. A nutritionist believes that the average daily calorie intake for adults in a city is more than 2000 calories. She surveys 25 residents to investigate.
